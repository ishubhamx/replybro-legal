Privacy Policy

ReplyBro  ·  Last updated: May 22, 2026

Overview

ReplyBro ("we", "our", "the app") is an automated notification-reply assistant for Android. We are committed to your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses, what stays on your device, and what (if anything) leaves it.

Short version: Your messages and reply rules never leave your device. ReplyBro has no server, no user accounts, and no cloud storage for your personal data.

1. Data We Access — and Where It Stays

ReplyBro uses Android's Notification Listener Service to read incoming notifications from WhatsApp so it can send automated replies on your behalf.

  • On device only Notification content (sender name, message text) — read in memory to match your rules and compose a reply. Never stored beyond the moment the reply is sent, except in the in-app reply log visible to you.
  • On device only Reply rules & settings — stored in the local app database (Room/SQLite) and SharedPreferences. Never synced or uploaded.
  • On device only Reply log — a history of sent replies stored locally so you can review them. Cleared when you uninstall the app.
  • On device only Wallet / diamonds balance — stored locally in DataStore. Not linked to any account or server.

The following third-party services are integrated in the app. Each operates under its own privacy policy.

We use anonymous, aggregated usage events (e.g. "rule created", "paywall opened") to understand how the app is used. No message content or personal data is included in any analytics event. Analytics is tied to a random app-instance ID, not to your name or Google account.

3. Permissions Explained

  • Notification access (NotificationListenerService) — Required to read incoming WhatsApp notifications and send replies via RemoteInput. You grant this explicitly in Android Settings. You can revoke it at any time; the app will stop working but your data is unaffected.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Used to show you status notifications (e.g. "reply sent", "service running").
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE — Required on Android 14+ to keep the reply service alive in the background. No extra data collection occurs.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — Allows the service to restart automatically after a reboot, so replies continue working.
  • INTERNET — Used only by Firebase (analytics, crash reporting) and AdMob. Not used to upload your messages or rules.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

All personal data (reply log, rules, settings, wallet balance) is stored solely on your device. To delete it completely, uninstall the app. You can also clear the reply log from within the app at any time.

Anonymous analytics and crash data retained by Google/Firebase is subject to their respective retention policies (typically 14 months for Analytics).

5. Children's Privacy

ReplyBro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can address it.
